Introduction

SmartSteth (RDL1) is an innovative health monitoring platform designed to provide real-time detection of respiratory symptoms such as pneumonia, asthma, and COPD. By combining cutting-edge machine learning with accessible design, SmartSteth empowers both patients and healthcare professionals to take early action and improve health outcomes.

Our Platform

The RDL1 platform bridges the gap between modern medical technology and underserved communities. By combining cloud-based analytics, edge AI, and user-friendly devices, SmartSteth delivers insights that save lives.
